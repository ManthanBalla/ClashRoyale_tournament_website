3. The Dual-Wallet System

Clash Arena operates a Dual-Wallet System to ensure financial transparency and platform integrity. Your wallet is divided into two distinct balances:

Deposit Balance

  • Funds added via Cashfree payment gateway.
  • Used exclusively to pay tournament entry fees and purchase Creator memberships.
  • Cannot be withdrawn directly to a bank account.
  • Non-refundable as cash; refunded only to this balance in eligible cases.

Winnings Balance

  • Funds earned exclusively from winning tournaments or creator revenue share.
  • This is the only balance eligible for withdrawal to your UPI account.
  • Subject to withdrawal limits and admin approval.
  • Minimum withdrawal amount: ₹100.

When paying an entry fee, the platform deducts from your Deposit Balance first. If insufficient, your Winnings Balance is used to cover the remaining amount.

4. Payments & Cashfree Integration

All financial transactions on Clash Arena are processed exclusively through Cashfree Payments, a licensed Payment Aggregator regulated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI).

  • By initiating a payment, you authorize Cashfree to process the transaction on behalf of Clash Arena.
  • Clash Arena does not store, process, or have access to your card numbers, CVV, or net-banking credentials.
  • Wallet credits are triggered only upon receiving a verified, HMAC-signed webhook confirmation from Cashfree's servers. The result displayed on your browser screen is not used to confirm payment.
  • In the event of a network failure after payment, your balance will be reconciled automatically within 24 hours via our daily reconciliation system.
  • All amounts are in Indian Rupees (INR).

6. Prize Distribution

Prize distribution for paid tournaments is automated and processed as a background task upon tournament completion. The prize pool is distributed as follows:

  • 70% — Credited to the Tournament Winner's Winnings Balance.
  • 18% — Credited to the Tournament Creator's Winnings Balance as a hosting fee.
  • 12% — Retained by the Platform as an operational/administrative fee.

Prize distribution is conditional upon the tournament reaching its minimum required number of participants. If the minimum is not met and the tournament is cancelled, all entry fees are fully refunded to participants' Deposit Balances.

Clash Arena reserves the right to modify the commission split with prior notice.
