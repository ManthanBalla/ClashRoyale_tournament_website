Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Last Updated: April 2026

Clash Arena is owned and operated by Manthan Kumar Balla.

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data when you use Clash Arena:

A. Account Information

  • Full name (as entered in profile)
  • Username / gaming alias
  • Email address
  • UPI ID (for withdrawal purposes only)
  • In-game username
  • Account creation date and last login time

B. Security & Fraud Detection Data

  • IP address (recorded on registration, login, and transactions)
  • Device fingerprint / user-agent string (browser and OS information)
  • Trust Score and flag status
  • Login attempt history

This data is collected solely to prevent multi-account fraud, automated abuse, and protect the financial integrity of the platform.

C. Financial & Transaction Data

  • Cashfree Order ID and Payment Reference ID
  • Payment amount, currency, status, and timestamp
  • Wallet balances (Deposit Balance and Winnings Balance)
  • Transaction history (credits, debits, refunds, winnings)
  • Withdrawal requests and approval status

We do NOT store, process, or have access to your card numbers, CVV codes, net banking credentials, or UPI PINs. All payment processing is handled exclusively by Cashfree Payments.

D. Platform Activity Data

  • Tournaments joined and created
  • Match results and uploaded proof screenshots
  • Dispute reports filed
  • Notifications received and read
  • Creators followed and notifications preferences

2. Why We Collect This Data

Every piece of data we collect serves a specific, legitimate purpose:

Data Type Purpose
Name, Email Account identification, password recovery, winner notifications
UPI ID Processing your withdrawal requests to your bank account
IP Address Multi-account detection, fraud prevention, security logging
Device Fingerprint Identifying shared-device abuse and suspicious access patterns
Payment Metadata Financial reconciliation, dispute resolution, refund processing
Transaction History Audit trail, financial compliance, user account transparency
Match Screenshots Result verification, dispute investigation

3. Payment Processing & Cashfree Security

Clash Arena integrates with Cashfree Payments, a PCI DSS compliant payment aggregator regulated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). The following applies to all payment data:

  • All payment pages and checkout flows are hosted and secured by Cashfree's infrastructure.
  • Clash Arena never sees or stores your card number, CVV, OTP, UPI PIN, or net banking password.
  • Payment confirmation is received via a server-to-server webhook secured with HMAC-SHA256 signature verification. We do not rely on client-side (browser) confirmations.
  • Cashfree's data practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy, available at cashfree.com.

4. Data Storage & Security

We are committed to protecting your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse:

  • All data is stored in a secure, encrypted PostgreSQL database hosted on a private server infrastructure.
  • Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel only (platform administrators).
  • All financial operations (wallet changes) are performed using atomic database transactions with row-level locking to prevent data corruption.
  • User passwords are hashed using Django's PBKDF2 algorithm and are never stored in plaintext.
  • Platform communications between users and the server use HTTPS (TLS encryption).
  • Rate limiting and automated fraud flagging are in place to prevent unauthorized access attempts.

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage users to use strong, unique passwords and to keep their account credentials private.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Cashfree Payments: Necessary transaction details (order amount, user reference) are shared to initiate and confirm payments.
  • Email Service Provider: Your email address is shared with our email service provider solely to send account-related communications (reward notifications, password resets, support responses).
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information if required to do so by a court order, law enforcement agency, or applicable Indian law (e.g., under the Information Technology Act, 2000).
  • Fraud Investigation: In the event of suspected fraud, data may be shared with relevant payment networks or cybercrime authorities.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for the duration of your account's existence and for a period thereafter as required by financial and legal regulations:

  • Account data is retained for the lifetime of your account.
  • Transaction and financial records are retained for a minimum of 7 years in compliance with Indian financial record-keeping requirements.
  • Upon account deletion, personally identifiable information is anonymized, but transaction records are retained for compliance.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Clash Arena uses session cookies to maintain your logged-in state. These are essential for the platform to function and do not track you across other websites. We do not use third-party advertising trackers or analytics platforms that profile you for advertising purposes.

8. Your Rights

As a user of Clash Arena, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a summary of personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may update your profile information (name, email, UPI ID, in-game username) at any time via your Profile page.
  • Deletion: You may request account deletion by contacting our support team. Note that transaction records will be anonymized and retained for compliance.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your transaction history in a structured format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact: connectwithmanthan12@gmail.com

9. Children's Privacy

Clash Arena is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that a minor has provided us with personal information without parental consent, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify registered users via email.
