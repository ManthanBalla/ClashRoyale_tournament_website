Help Center

Quick guide for Players, Creators, and Admins.

Player Checklist

  • • Complete profile: full name, email, and UPI ID.
  • • Join tournament from home list, then follow status updates.
  • • For paid tournaments, ensure wallet balance before joining.
  • • Use dispute form if result or fairness issue appears.
  • • Check notifications for results, rewards, and refunds.

Creator Checklist

  • • Activate creator plan, then host tournament with correct details.
  • • Set rules clearly and verify start/end timings.
  • • Upload leaderboard and reward proof after completion.
  • • Open Send Rewards, choose codes + players, then dispatch.
  • • Verify winner profile details (name, email, UPI) before payout.

Admin Checklist

  • • Monitor tournaments, disputes, and membership compliance.
  • • Process withdrawals and maintain transaction consistency.
  • • Manage user roles responsibly (promote/demote/ban).
  • • Enforce fair play and policy standards platform-wide.
  • • Review notifications and reports daily for quick resolution.
