How Money Works

We use a strict Dual-Wallet System to protect our platform and ensure fair play.

Deposit Balance

  • Money you add via Cashfree.
  • Used exclusively to pay for tournament entry fees.
  • Cannot be withdrawn to bank.

Winnings Balance

  • Money earned strictly by winning tournaments.
  • Can be withdrawn directly to your UPI.

Why split the wallet? This prevents financial misuse (like money laundering) and guarantees that the platform is used for its intended purpose: competitive gaming.

➕

How to Add Money

  • ➔ Click the "Add Money" button in your profile.
  • ➔ Enter the desired amount.
  • ➔ Pay securely via Cashfree using UPI, Cards, or Netbanking.
  • ➔ Your payment is verified instantly via secure servers.
  • ➔ Wallet is credited automatically.

Note: Sometimes it may take a few seconds to update your balance while we securely verify the payment directly with the bank.

🏆

How Paid Tournaments Work

1. Entry Fees & Prize Pool

Every player pays a small entry fee to join. All entry fees combine to form the total prize pool.

Example: 10 players × ₹50 entry = ₹500 Prize Pool

2. Reward Distribution

Prizes are automatically calculated and split securely:

🥇 70% To the Winner (₹350)
👑 18% To the Creator (₹90)
⚙️ 12% Platform Fee (₹60)

3. Who Holds the Money?

The Platform holds all funds securely. Creators and hosts never touch your entry fees. When the tournament ends, our automated system distributes the rewards safely to everyone's wallet.

How to Withdraw Money

  1. Go to the Withdraw page from your profile.
  2. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw (Minimum ₹100).
  3. Enter your correct UPI ID.
  4. Submit the request.
  5. An admin will quickly review the request for security.
  6. Money is sent directly to your bank account.
Important Rules: You can only withdraw from your Winnings Balance. Withdrawals undergo manual review for safety, which may take up to 24-48 hours depending on volume.

Common Questions (FAQ)

Q: Why is my money split into two balances?

A: To ensure fair play, prevent money laundering, and secure withdrawals strictly for actual tournament winners.

Q: I paid but money is not added?

A: It may take a few seconds due to secure server-to-server verification. If an issue persists, our daily automated reconciliation task will fix it.

Q: Can I withdraw my deposit money?

A: No. Only funds in the Winnings Balance can be withdrawn. Deposit money is strictly for joining tournaments.

Q: What if a tournament is cancelled?

A: Your entry fee is automatically and instantly refunded to your Deposit Balance.

Q: How long does withdrawal take?

A: It depends on administrative approval and banking processing times, but typically within 1-3 business days.
