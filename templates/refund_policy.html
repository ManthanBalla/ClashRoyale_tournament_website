Refund Policy

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Last Updated: April 2026

Clash Arena is owned and operated by Manthan Kumar Balla.

This policy governs all refund and cancellation matters on the Clash Arena platform. Please read it carefully before making any deposit or joining a paid tournament.

1. Understanding the Dual-Wallet System

Before reading this policy, it is essential to understand how our wallet works, as it directly determines refund eligibility.

Deposit Balance

Money added via Cashfree. Used to pay entry fees. This balance cannot be withdrawn as cash. In approved refund cases, it is credited back to this balance — not to your bank account.

Winnings Balance

Money earned from winning tournaments. This is the only balance withdrawable to your UPI account. Not subject to refund policies — this is your earned income.

Important: Refunds are always credited to your Deposit Balance, not to your original payment method or bank account. Clash Arena does not reverse payments to external bank accounts except in cases of duplicate or fraudulent charges confirmed by Cashfree.

2. Cases Where Refunds Are Granted

Refunds are provided automatically or upon request in the following specific circumstances:

✓

Tournament Cancelled by Creator

If the tournament organizer cancels the event before it begins, 100% of the entry fee is automatically refunded to all participants' Deposit Balances. This is processed instantly by the system.

✓

Tournament Does Not Meet Minimum Players

If a tournament fails to attract the required minimum number of participants and is subsequently cancelled, all entry fees are automatically refunded to participants' Deposit Balances.

✓

Platform Technical Failure

If a verified, documented technical failure on Clash Arena's side (not your device or internet) prevented you from participating in a tournament you had already joined and paid for, a refund may be issued upon review. Contact support within 24 hours of the incident with evidence.

✓

Duplicate or Erroneous Payment

If your account is charged more than once for a single transaction due to a payment gateway error, the duplicate amount is refunded to your Deposit Balance within 24 hours after confirmation from Cashfree. For external bank refunds, please raise a dispute with Cashfree directly.

3. Cases Where Refunds Are NOT Granted

To protect the fairness of the prize pool for all participants, refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

✗

Deposit Balance Withdrawal Request

Funds added to your Deposit Balance via Cashfree cannot be withdrawn to a bank account. This balance is strictly for platform activity (entry fees, memberships).

✗

Voluntary Exit After Joining

If you join a paid tournament and later decide not to participate, no refund is issued. Entry fees are locked into the prize pool upon joining.

✗

Tournament Has Already Started

Once a tournament transitions to "Ongoing" status, entry fees are non-refundable regardless of your participation or outcome.

✗

Disqualification Due to Rule Violation

If you are removed from a tournament for cheating, abusive behaviour, failing to submit results, or any rule violation, no refund will be provided.

✗

Account Suspension for Fraud

If your account is suspended for multi-accounting, fraud, or any terms violation, all wallet balances are forfeited and no refunds are issued.

✗

Membership / Subscription Fees

Creator membership plans are non-refundable once activated, as access to hosting features is granted immediately upon payment confirmation.

✗

Internet or Device Issues on Your End

Clash Arena is not responsible for connectivity or hardware failures on your side that affect your ability to participate in a tournament.

4. How Refunds Are Processed

⚡

Refund Destination

All refunds are credited to your Deposit Balance — not to your bank or UPI.

🕐

Processing Time

Automated refunds (cancelled tournaments) are instant. Manual refunds processed within 24 hours.

📧

Refund Request

For non-automatic cases, contact support at connectwithmanthan12@gmail.com with evidence.

5. Withdrawals vs. Refunds — Key Distinction

These are two separate processes and must not be confused:

Aspect Refund Withdrawal
Source Balance Deposit Balance Winnings Balance only
Destination Back to Deposit Balance Your UPI account
Trigger Platform/system event User-initiated request
Admin Approval Required (manual cases) Always required

6. Contact for Refund Requests

If you believe you qualify for a refund under the conditions outlined above, please contact our support team with your username, the tournament name, and supporting evidence.

Email: connectwithmanthan12@gmail.com
