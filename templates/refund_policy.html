Refund Policy

Wallet & tournament refunds

Wallet top-ups are non-refundable once successfully credited to your Clash Arena wallet.

Entry fee refunds are automatic when a paid tournament is cancelled or fails minimum-player criteria.

If a withdrawal is rejected by admin, the full amount is returned to your wallet automatically.

Reward disputes are reviewed only when valid evidence is submitted within the tournament dispute window.

Chargeback abuse or fake refund claims can result in permanent account suspension.
