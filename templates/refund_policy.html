Refund Policy

Clash Arena is owned and operated by Manthan Kumar Balla.

We strive to provide a seamless and fair tournament experience. However, we understand that issues may occasionally arise. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are provided.

1. Refund Conditions

Refunds for tournament entry fees will only be processed under the following circumstances:

  • Tournament Cancelled: If the tournament organizer cancels the tournament before it begins, all participants will receive a full automated refund.
  • Technical Issue: If a proven platform technical issue prevented you from participating, and you report it with evidence before the tournament starts.

2. No Refund Cases

To ensure fairness and prize pool integrity for all players, refunds will not be issued in the following scenarios:

  • User Joins and Leaves: If you join a paid tournament and then voluntarily decide to leave or not participate.
  • Match is Completed: Once the tournament has started or the match has been completed, entry fees are strictly non-refundable as they contribute to the winner's prize pool.
  • Disqualification: If you are disqualified for cheating, breaking the rules, or failing to show up on time.

3. Processing Time

If your refund request is approved, the funds will be credited back to your original payment method. Please note that refunds are processed within 5–7 business days depending on your bank.

4. Contact Us

If you believe you are entitled to a refund based on the conditions above, please reach out to our support team immediately.

Email support for refund issues: connectwithmanthan12@gmail.com
